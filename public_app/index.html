About

Hi there and welcome!

My name is Julia and I live in New York. What can I say, I love this city endlessly! Its architecture, streets, people, vibe, and energy motivate and inspire me every day. It is there for you when you are happy and sad, it celebrates your wins and loses, it picks you up and lets you go when you need it. Without any doubt, it is the best place I’ve ever lived.

This project is my Ode to New York where I collect images and sounds of the city. All content including pictures and audio files was created by me using iPhone 6 and Pixel 2 phones. The sounds recorded on the Pixel 2 phone as well using Moment Videomicro microphone.

To listen to the sound, click on the volume icon on the image on the Home page. You can also click on the image to expand it and close it by clicking on it again. As with any ambient sound, it is recommended that you use headphones for a better experience.

I hope you enjoy the Sounds of New York as much as I do. Maybe you used to live here before and miss it? This is a great opportunity to "revisit" the city again. And maybe you've never been here before in which case, welcome and have fun! Feel free to drop me a line or two at any time. I would love to hear from you.

From New York City with love,
Julia
